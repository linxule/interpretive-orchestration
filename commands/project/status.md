## Readiness Signals

### Stage 1 ‚Üí Stage 2 Transition

**You're ready when:**
- ‚úì 10-15 documents manually coded (minimum)
- ‚úì Initial data structure with clear concept definitions
- ‚úì 5+ analytical memos written
- ‚úì You can articulate YOUR theoretical sensitivity
- ‚úì You feel interpretive foundation is established

**How to know:**
> "I have a feel for my data. I know what patterns matter to ME.
> I can distinguish between superficial and theoretically significant.
> I have categories that emerged from MY engagement."

**Not ready if:**
- You're still discovering what your data is about
- Concepts feel vague or unclear
- You're uncertain about theoretical boundaries
- You haven't written memos about emerging insights

**Guidance:** Don't rush! Stage 1 depth is irreplaceable.

### Stage 2 ‚Üí Stage 3 Transition

**You're ready when:**
- ‚úì All (or most) documents coded
- ‚úì Data structure refined through feedback rounds
- ‚úì Pattern characterization complete (if applicable)
- ‚úì You have AI-organized evidence tables
- ‚úì You're ready to THEORIZE (not just organize)

**How to know:**
> "I have all the organized evidence I need. Now I need to
> construct theoretical meaning, integrate with literature,
> and articulate my contribution."

**Not ready if:**
- Still discovering new patterns
- Framework feels unstable
- More documents would add theoretical insight
- Haven't achieved theoretical saturation
